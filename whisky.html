Dewar’s Guided Tour & Whiskey Tasting

Tours of our traditional distillery take place throughout the day, and include options for whisky aficionados. All tickets include access to the museum and warehouse experience.

  • Guided distillery tours
  • Interactive Heritage Exhibition
  • Whisky Lounge and cafe which serves freshly prepared lunches using the finest local produce including soup, thick fill sandwiches and afternoon tea.
  • Fully licensed bar with a range of Scotch whiskies and tasting flights.
  • Distillery shop showcases the full range from John Dewar & Sons Fine Whisky Emporium, along with our exclusive single cask ‘fill your own’ bottle.
  • The heritage museum, tasting bar, shop and café are all on one level with toilets accessed via a lift.
  • Full allergen information is available in the café.
  • The production area tour distance is 405 metres including 61 steps. They offer an alternative route including the still house and warehouse which is entirely flat.
  • All visitors have a safe and enjoyable visit regardless of their access requirements.
